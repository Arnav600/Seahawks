Go Seahawks!

The Seahawks are one of the best teams in the NFL. They are in the NFC West and are located in the Rainy City, Seattle. They play at Century-Link Stadium, and have a very strong fanbase. Their General Manager is John Schneider and their head coach is Pete Caroll. Russell Wilson, the quarterback, leads the offense and has led the Seahawks to 8 straight winning seasons along with a superbowl win. They also have a stingy defense led by Bobby Wagner.
